**Work in progress.**

## Research Themes

- Smart Radio Environments (SREs)
- Near-field communications, sensing, and imaging
- Reconfigurable Intelligent Surfaces (RIS)
- XL-MIMO and holographic MIMO systems
- Electromagnetic signal processing for 6G

## Ongoing and Recent Projects

- **RESTART (Structural Project S12):** communications, localization, and sensing in SREs.
- **TIMES (H2020):** multi-goal mesh network optimization and smart propagation environments.
- **6G-SHINE (H2020):** PHY and MAC enablers and RIS models for in-X subnetworks.

## Current Research Directions

- Near-field MU-MIMO architectures with active feeder and RIS-aided beamfocusing.
- Holographic imaging design with XL-MIMO and RIS in NLOS scenarios.
- OAM-based communications with stacked intelligent metasurfaces.
