I am a telecommunications researcher focused on **6G Smart Radio Environments**, with interests in near-field processing, XL-MIMO, RIS-enabled architectures, and wireless imaging.

My research activity combines theory and simulation for next-generation wireless systems, with a strong focus on practical impact through EU and national projects.

## Research Interests

- Smart Radio Environments (SREs)
- Near-field communications and sensing
- Reconfigurable Intelligent Surfaces (RIS)
- Holographic and XL-MIMO systems
- Over-the-air electromagnetic signal processing

## Current Focus

- Architectures and algorithms for multi-user near-field MIMO in 6G systems
- RIS-aided beam focusing and imaging in NLOS scenarios
- Cross-project technical coordination in international research collaborations
